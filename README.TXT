
XWIKI RECRUITMENT MANAGER
-------------------------

Author: XWiki SAS
Licence: LGPL

The XWiki Recruitment Manager is an XWiki application allowing to manager job candidates and post their Resume and reviews on your XWiki installation.

The Recruitment Manager is compatible with XWiki 2.x and has been tested on XWiki 2.0 and 2.0.1.

To build use maven "mvn install". This will produce a XAR that you can import in your XWiki installation.

Visit RecruitmentCode.Install to finish the installation.
